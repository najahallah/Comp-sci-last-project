How to play ASSASSIN

Assassin (also Gotcha, Assassins, KAOS (Killing As Organized Sport), Juggernaut, Battle Royal, Paranoia, Killer, Elimination, or Circle of Death) is a live-action game. Players try to eliminate each other from the game using mock weapons in an effort to become the last surviving player. After registering, you will be assigned a target to "kill". In this version of the game, you must kill any and all members of the game. The last one standing wins. All players must follow the Rules of Conduct below to play.

Rules of Conduct

  1. Each person for themselves. You may team up, but remember. Keep your friends close, but your enemies closer.
  2. If you decide to team up, only you may execute your target. If your accomplice kills your target, the death does not count.
  3. The only accepted ways to "kill" someone are:
    • "Poison": Salt their water
    • "Bomb": Stick a timer on their seat or in their bag
    • "Sniper": Hit them with a water in the neck (only allowed outside of school). Other body parts hit with water do not count. NOTE: Water guns, and other similar weapons are prohibited on school property.
  4. If you are killed, you must give your name and PIN to your killer.
  5. Use the honor system. Don't be a butt and give the wrong pin or deny being killed.
  6. Have fun and stay safe!

Steps to play

  1. Register Here.
    • Enter your email, name, password, and a four digit pin. (DO NOT SHARE YOUR PIN WITH ANYONE ELSE!)
    • Click "Create".
  2. Go to sign in and there, you will receive the name of your target. Write down the name of your target. Do not tell your target that you are hunting them.
  3. Follow the Rules of Conduct above.

What to do after you have successfully killed your target.

  1. Log in first.
  2. Then go to the "Kills" page.
  3. Enter your target's PIN. (Yay! You assassinated them!)
  4. Click "Assassinate". Then get your new target by pressing the appropriate button. Try not to be killed in the meantime.
