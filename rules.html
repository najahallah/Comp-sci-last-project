How to play ASSASSIN

Assassin (also Gotcha, Assassins, KAOS (Killing as organized sport), Juggernaut, Battle Royal, Paranoia, Killer, Elimination, or Circle of Death) is a live-action game. Players try to eliminate each other from the game using mock weapons in an effort to become the last surviving player. After registering, you will be assigned a target to "kill". In this version of the game, players must kill only once within 3 days in order to move on to the next round. All players must follow the Rules of Conduct below to play.

Rules of Conduct

Steps to play

  1. Register on this site
    • Click "Register" on the menu to the left.
    • Enter your First and Last name and click "Submit".
  2. Your PIN number for the game will pop up. Write it down!
  3. You will also receive the name of your target. Write down the name of your target.
  4. Follow the Rules of Conduct above.

Moving on to the next round

  1. Go to the "Kills" page.
  2. Enter your name, your PIN, your target's name, and your target's PIN.
  3. Click "Submit". Try not to be killed in the mean time.
