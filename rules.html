How to play ASSASSIN

Assassin (also Gotcha, Assassins, KAOS (Killing as organized sport), Juggernaut, Battle Royal, Paranoia, Killer, Elimination, or Circle of Death) is a live-action game. Players try to eliminate each other from the game using mock weapons in an effort to become the last surviving player. After registering, you will be assigned a target to "kill". In this version of the game, players must kill only once within 3 days (one round) in order to move on to the next round. All players must follow the Rules of Conduct below to play.

Rules of Conduct

  1. You may only "kill" one person (your target) per round
  2. The only accepted ways to "kill" someone are:
    • "Poison": Salt their water
    • "Bomb": Stick a timer on their seat or in their bag
    • "Sniper": Hit them with a water in the neck (only allowed outside of school). Other body parts hit with water do not count.
  3. If you are killed, you must give your name and PIN to your killer
  4. Use the honor system
  5. Have fun and stay safe!

Steps to play

  1. Register on this site
    • Click Register
    • Enter your First and Last name and click "Submit".
  2. Your PIN number for the game will pop up. Write it down!
  3. You will also receive the name of your target. Write down the name of your target.
  4. Follow the Rules of Conduct above.

Moving on to the next round

  1. Go to the "Kills" page.
  2. Enter your name, your PIN, your target's name, and your target's PIN.
  3. Click "Submit". Try not to be killed in the mean time.
